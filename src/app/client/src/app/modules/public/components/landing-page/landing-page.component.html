The Togolese Government has undertaken an important task of digital transforming the educational landscape and bringing about large-scale improvement across the schools of the country. Considering the impact of COVID-19 pandemic, a shift from traditional classroom transactions to digital education solutions is imperative. There is a need for a viable, feasible and sustainable digital education solution for the country. In this context, the Government of Togo has established a digital platform to enable students and teachers across the country to access a variety of teaching and learning resources. This platform will support in learning as per the school curriculum complemented with certain specialized skills which are not included in the current school curriculum.

The platform leverages open source technology and the underlying policies and tools will empower the entire education ecosystem of the country- educationists, experts, organizations, institutions(- government, autonomous, non-government and private) to participate, contribute, and achieve learning goals at scale for the country
